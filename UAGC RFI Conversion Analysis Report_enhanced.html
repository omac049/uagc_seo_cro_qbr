While sessions grew modestly (+6.9%), the efficiency gains are significant: visitors completed the RFI process faster (-14% session duration) and at higher rates.

Key Insights & Recommendations

What Worked
  • 62% of pages (36/58) saw conversion rate improvements
  • Health-related programs showed strongest gains
  • Pre-selection eliminated friction for complex program names
  • Users completed forms 14% faster
Areas for Investigation
  • Early childhood education programs (non-bachelor's) declined significantly
  • Liberal arts and certificate programs underperformed
  • Pre-selection may not align with user expectations for some programs
  • Different audiences may have different form preferences
Priority Recommendations
1
Refine Pre-selection Logic

Log instances where users change pre-selected programs to identify mismatch patterns

2
Customize Underperforming Pages

Develop tailored content for early childhood and liberal arts programs

3
Scale Success Patterns

Apply health program optimizations to similar high-complexity programs
